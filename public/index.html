Garlic Shrimp Stir-Fry


Ingredients:

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1/2 cup carrots, julienned
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Instructions:

  1. 1. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, oyster sauce, cornstarch, red pepper flakes, and black pepper. Set aside.
  2. 2. Heat oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. 3. Add shrimp and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until they turn pink and are cooked through. Remove shrimp from the skillet and set aside.
  4. 4. In the same skillet, add bell pepper, snap peas, and carrots. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  5. 5. Return the shrimp to the skillet. Pour the sauce over the shrimp and vegetables. Stir well to coat everything evenly.
  6. 6. Continue cooking for another 2 minutes until the sauce thickens and coats the ingredients.
  7. 7. Remove from heat and stir in the chopped green onions.
  8. 8. Serve the garlic shrimp stir-fry over cooked rice.
  9. 9. Enjoy!
